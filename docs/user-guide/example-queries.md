# Example queries

These prompts tend to produce higher-quality itineraries because they provide clear constraints.

## Trip basics

- `Plan a 5-day trip to Goa for 2 people in April. Budget: ₹60,000. Prefer beaches + cafes + nightlife.`
- `Plan a 3-day trip to Manali in winter for a couple. Avoid long hikes.`
- `Weekend getaway from Mumbai (2 nights). Prefer nature and good food.`

## With constraints

- `Plan a 6-day Japan trip (Tokyo + Kyoto). Vegetarian-friendly. Moderate walking only.`
- `Plan a 4-day Dubai trip for a family with kids. Include indoor options in case of heat.`
- `Plan a 7-day Europe trip with a budget of $2500. Focus on museums and public transport.`

## Budgeting-focused

- `Plan a 5-day trip to Bali. Break down hotel + food + local transport + activities + buffer. Convert totals to INR.`
- `Plan a 3-day trip to Pune with a tight budget. Recommend low-cost stays and free attractions.`

## Troubleshooting prompts

If a place is ambiguous, include the country/state:

- `Plan a 3-day trip to Springfield, Illinois, USA.`
